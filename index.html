Welcome

Haven Recording was established in 2005 in Houston, TX, and relocated to Los Angeles in 2011. We are centrally located in the Los Angeles Arts District.

Key features of the facility include a spacious Lobby with TV, Xbox, and Kitchenette. We offer spacious live and control rooms that can comfortably accommodate large groups. In addition to the main tracking room, we have a large Iso/vocal booth. The lobby is wired for sound as well, so you can utilize the unique reflective sound of the raw cedar and brick walls.

The studio's one-of-a-kind layout and comfortable aesthetic make for an excellent environment to rehearse, write, track, or mix.

Booking & Events

The studio is available for rent on its own to clients who have their own engineers, or with one of our house engineers/assistant engineers for an additional charge. Additionally, the studio can be rented out for small private events, film/photo shoots, artist showcases, album releases, and press events.
