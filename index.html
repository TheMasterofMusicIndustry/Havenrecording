Our Story

Established in 2005 and relocated to the vibrant Los Angeles Arts District in 2011, Haven Recording was built with one goal: to provide a comfortable, professional environment where creativity can thrive.

Our unique facility combines a spacious live room and control room with a relaxed aesthetic, making it the perfect environment to rehearse, write, track, or mix your next project.

The Facility

We can comfortably accommodate large groups. In addition to the main tracking room, we feature a large Iso/vocal booth.

Our lobby is wired for sound, allowing you to utilize the unique, reflective sonic character of its raw cedar and brick walls.

  • Spacious Live & Control Rooms
  • Large Iso/Vocal Booth
  • Lobby Wired for Re-amping
  • Client Lounge with Kitchenette
  • Available for Private Events & Shoots
